About News & Insights

ealtor.com’s News & Insights is a unique destination where we aim to provide readers with timely insights on the housing market that they can’t get anywhere else. Our mission is to empower buyers, sellers, renters and homeowners with the knowledge they need to find their way home.

Who We Are

News & Insights is a dedicated team of journalists and economists who provide accurate, impartial, and insightful information steeped in data. Our different backgrounds allow us to cover a wide range of topics and perspectives and ensure our coverage is relevant and accurate.

Our Vision

To become a trusted regional real estate leader in Cambodia and neighboring countries, delivering modern, reliable, and value-driven property solutions that improve communities and create sustainable opportunities for everyone.

Our Mission

We specialize in delivering comprehensive news and analysis on various aspects of real estate: News you need to navigate change and take the next step toward your current and future home. Entertainment to enjoy a glimpse into unique and celebrity homes. Buying and selling: From market trends to negotiations, we provide valuable insights for first-time and seasoned buyers and sellers. Renting: Whether you’re looking for your next rental or confused by lease agreements, our resources can help you make informed decisions. Owning: We cover maintenance tips, home improvement ideas, and financial advice to help you maximize your investment now and whenever it’s time to sell. Mortgages: Understanding financing is crucial. We break down mortgage types, interest rates, and lending processes to help you get the best deal.

Our Commitment

We are committed to journalistic integrity. We present unbiased information. When we make a mistake, we correct it. To submit a correction, email news@realtor.com.

Join Our Community

Whether you have questions about a specific topic or want to share your own experiences in the real estate market, we want to hear from you at news@realtor.com Follow us on Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn and install our mobile app for the latest updates and expert advice.
